EX-CHANGE is a publication and exhibition celebrating the work of the Carnegie Mellon University School of Architecture from 1st year to PhD. Inaugurated in 2017, EX-CHANGE represents an ongoing opportunity to shine new light on the SoA's programs and to position the work within larger questions of research and practice. You can find out more about past EX-CHANGE years here.

The global pandemic of 2020 has made it impossible to gather our large community together in person, so our small but mighty team has rallied to produce the website you see here.
